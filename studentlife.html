Student Life

Student Council

Student Council is a small group of dedicated students that do their very best to promote school spirit. They organize many different activites to make your high school days memorable and fun.

Robotics

Students in robotics are taught to build remote control robots. They also take part in big competetions with many schools to see whos got the best bot.

Garden Project

To help make the school yard a beautiful place that students will want to hangout at with their friends, students and teachers have gotten together to plant and build rock paths. Not only will it make the school more beautiful, but it will give the cafeteria the chance to grow their own fruits and vegetables.

Radio Show

The Voice is LHAs personal public radio station hosted by students and sometimes with teachers. They play music and have fun little talks everyday at school in the morning and during lunch.
