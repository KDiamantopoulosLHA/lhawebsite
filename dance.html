Dance

The dance program has two goals: firstly, to instill the discipline and commitment required to become a good dancer; and secondly, to foster a lifetime appreciation for the arts. In addition to the regular classes in the day-program, there is an after-school dance program, classes run every day of the week. In our after school dance program, they have teach a variety of dance styles. Some dance styles students are taught are; Hip Hop, Broadway, Ballet, Africain, Tap, Balladi, Salsa, Moderne and Contemporary.
