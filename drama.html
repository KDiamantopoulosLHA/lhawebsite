Drama

Few other activities allow students to discover who they are and how to relate to others than the performing arts. Through the study of drama, students have a fun and challenging experience while building self-esteem, oral-communication and public-speaking skills. LaurenHill Academy offers Drama as a curricular program to all students and Drama Club as an extra-curricular program to students. The students in drama club must audition and will spend most of the year putting together a perfomance for all students and parents to come see.
